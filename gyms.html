Sinnoh Region Gyms

There are a total of eight gyms present in the region, all which need to be be conquered in order to acquire the eight gym badges needed to take on the Elite Four in the Pokémon League and "complete" the game. Below is a list of each Gym and their Leader, later on I will go into more detail about each gym.

  1. Oreburgh City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Roark
    • Specialty: Rock-type
  2. Eterna City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Gardenia
    • Specialty: Grass-type
  3. Veilstone City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Maylene
    • Specialty: Fighting-type
  4. Pastoria City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Crasher Wake
    • Specialty: Water-type
  5. Heathome City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Fantina
    • Specialty: Ghost-type
  6. Canalave City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Byron
    • Specialty: Steel-type
  7. Snowpoint City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Candice
    • Specialty: Ice-type
  8. Sunnyshore City Gym:
    • Gym Leader: Volkner
    • Specialty: Electric-type

Gym 1: Oreburgh City

Sinnoh's first gym is located in Oreburgh City is found after visiting Jubilife City and by then following Route 203 into the Oreburgh Cave. This gym unlike some others has no puzzles or the like, and just consists of a couple trainers that can be bypassed through the use of the bridges present in the gym. Once you reach the end of the gym you'll be able to challenge Roark.

Gym 2: Eterna City

The second gym can be accessed the moment you arrive in Eterna City, unlike the previous gym ths once does possess a puzzle. You are unable to battle Gardenia until you have completed the puzzle, Each trainer you battle in this gym will give you a hint as to where the next trainer is located. Once they have all been defeated you can challenge Gardenia.

Gym 3: Veilstone City

The gym is accessible once you arrive in Veilstone City, similarly to the gym present in Eterna City this gym also has a puzzle that must be completed in order to challenge the gym leader. The player must move various boards around in order to create a path to Maylene, you will need to also defeat trainers along the way.

Gym 4: Pastoria City

The gym in Pastoria is located in the south of the city, this gym has a simple water puzzle which must be completed in order to challenge Crasher Wake. The player solves this puzzle by raising or lowering the water level in the gym and defeating trainers as you progress through the area. As usual at the end of the gym you will able to challenge the gym leader, Crasher Wake in this instance.

Gym 5: Hearthome City

While the gym in Hearthome city will be the 5th that you will challenge, it is actually the third gym you come across on your journey in Sinnoh. The puzzle/gimmick of this gym is that you will be asked a simple maths question and must enter the door that corresponds to the correct answer to get to the next floor. At the end of the gym is Fantina, the leader waiting for your challenge.

Gym 6: Canalave City

Once you reach Canalave City you can access the gym, it is run by Byron, Roark's father. This gyms puzzle is all about finding a path to Byron, this is done by utilizing various small elevators and moving platforms in the gym and as usual you will be battling trainers along the way. If you choose Chimchar as your starter and still have him actively in your party this gym will likely be very easy.

Gym 7: Snowpoint City

In this gym you will have to clear a path to the leader Candice by sliding down the various platforms of the gmy, destroying snowballs as you go. This is done by sliding on ice panels and using other snowballs and trainers to bounce off.

The final gym of the game found in Sunnyshore City, upon first arriving in the city some story will need to be played out before you can take on the gym. Players will need to forge their path through the gym by rotating various gears to make a route to the leaker.
