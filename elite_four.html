The Champion: Cynthia | Specialty: None

The Elite Four and the Champion this time around are pretty tough and will require you to either have very over-levelled Pokémon and to make use of items, or you will need to go with a strategic Pokémon setup to take advantage of type-advantages. Each opponent you will face here has extremely well tuned Pokémon, they have well rolled IVs and EVs as well as having top tier held items no the mention the consumables their trainers will use. Below I will include Pokémon suggestions for each Elite-Four member and the Champion as well as a team setup I suggest but seeing as you can only have a total of six Pokémon going in to battle all the Elite-Four and the Champion it will be up to you to choose your final setup that you think will be able to defeat them. You also may just want to go into battle with your favourite Pokémon as opposed to a "overpowered" team comp.

Example Team for the Pokémon League

Member 1: Aaron

For this battle I suggest you bring a Staraptor, Rapidash, and a Garchomp as part of team setup for this battle, as they cover the weaknesses of Aaron's Pokémon who are a primarily Bug Hybrid Types.

Flint's specialty may be Fire Type but his team only actually has 2 fire-type Pokémon, with the other three Pokémon each having different types. Suggest Pokémon for this battle include Gyarados, Staraptor, Luxray, and Gastrodon.

Member 4: Lucian

Unlike Flint, Lucian's Pokémon are all the type of his specialty Psychic. Making a counter-setup easier to get going. The Pokémon best suited to handling Lucian's team are Gyarados, Gengar, and Garchomp.

The Champion: Cynthia

Unlike the members of the Elite-Four Cynthia does not have a type specialty and instead has a nice spread of types. Even so some Pokémon cover multiple type advantages against her team, I would suggest going in with Gyarados, Staraptor, and Gastrodon.
